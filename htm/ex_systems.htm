Systems Management

In addition to the mechanical control aspects of piloting a VideoRay, VideoRay Cockpit has many additional features to assist the pilot. These features are accessed through the VideoRay Cockpit interface and can be grouped in the following categories.

  • Interface tuning to facilitate operating in specific conditions
  • Control of non-piloting features
    • Camera settings
    • Collect still images and videos
  • Provide systems status information
