Exercise: Tether Management

Objective

This exercise helps participants learn how to coil a tether neatly so that it can be unspooled easily and without kinks.

Preparation

Disconnect the tether from the ROV and control panel. Uncoil at least 20 m of tether.

Techniques

Method A) Coil the tether using the over and under technique. In this technique, the free end of the tether goes over for the first loop (1), and under for the second loop (1), and alternates over and under for each subsequent loop.

Method B) Coil the tether using the figure 8 technique. In this technique, the tether is laid in alternate left and right loops in the shape of a figure 8. The tether can be carried like this or folded with one set of loops on the other.

Tips

  • Remove any twists in the tether before beginning to coil it.
  • Try to keep each coil loop the same size.
