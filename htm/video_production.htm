Video Production

One of the most common requirements for ROV projects is to provide a video record of the underwater activity or object of interest. Often, even though the project might involve hours of on-site operations, the critical data might only be on-screen for a few minutes, or even be just one snapshot. Being able to reduce the hours and megabytes of data down to something more manageable, and being able to distribute this information is a baseline requirement.
