Exercise: Depth Gauge

Objective

This exercise helps participants learn how to use the depth gauge to determine the depth of the ROV and control its depth with precision.

Preparation

Technique

Submerge the ROV and observe the depth gauge while it submerges. Continue to submerge the ROV to the bottom and determine the depth. Select a depth and pilot the ROV to that depth. Pilot the ROV at that depth for about 5 - 10 meters.

Tips

  • Near neutral buoyancy is helpful in maintaining a specific depth.
  • The depth scale can be changed to match the anticipated depth range and make the scale easier to read, especially for shallow depth ranges.
