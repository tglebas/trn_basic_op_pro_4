Exercise: Lights Control

Objective

This exercise helps participants learn how to adjust the lights to get the best image quality.

Technique

Adjust lights from dim to bright with the ROV in different locations (surface, submerged, in open water and close to an object) and notice effects on the image quality.

Tips

  • More light does not always result in the best image quality.
  • The camera and digital interface have numerous settings that can also improve image quality.
