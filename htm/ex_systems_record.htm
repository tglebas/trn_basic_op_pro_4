Exercise: Video Recording

Video Recordings can be captured using the Video Record button on the hand controller. Videos are stored on the hard drive.

Use the Video Record button to record several videos while completing the previous exercises.

Video Recording Tips

  • Use the Text Overlay and capture still images while recording to create "index" frames that can be used to quickly navigate to specific sections of the recorded video.
  • Turn on the Text Overlay at the beginning of the recording and to document specific highlights, but turn it off otherwise.
  • Record a few seconds of video on the topside before commencing the dive - underwater video generally all looks the same.
