Course Objectives


Photo courtesy of Tom Glebas

  • Be aware of risks and safety concerns associated with ROV operations
  • Know and understand the system's features, capabilities and limitations
  • Know the roles and responsibilities of a typical ROV crew
  • Know how to care for and maintain the system
  • Be able to pilot in easy to moderate conditions
    • Low current
    • Reasonable visibility
    • Moderate depth
  • Become competent and confident in the use of the VideoRay Pro 4
  • Become aware of where to go from here...
  • Oh, yeah... Get to wear the T-shirt!
