Assessments

Each exercise includes an assessment rubric. Participants' performances will be evaluated based on the criteria outlined in the specific knowledge topic or skill rubric.

Example - Pass / Fail Task

Skill Fail Pass Score
Objective Unable to meet the expectations Able to meet the expectations P / F
Inspect a cartridge seal Unable to determine if a cartridge seal is acceptable for use or not Able to determine if a cartridge seal is acceptable for use or not P / F

Example - Scale Graded Task*

Skill 0 1 2 3 4 5 Score
Objective Unable to complete any portion of the task Able to complete less than 50% of the task Able to complete the majority of the task, but not 100% or not in one attempt Able to complete the task, but with some difficulty and occasional errors Able to complete the task with moderate speed and proficiency and minimal errors Able to complete the task skill with speed, proficiency & without any errors 0 - 5
Follow a vertical line Unable to locate the line or follow it for any distance Able to locate the line, but cannot follow it for more than a short distance Able to follow the line briefly, but not the entire length in one attempt Able to follow the line but not at a steady speed or standoff distance Able to follow the line, with only minor changes in speed or standoff distance Able to follow the line at a steady speed and standoff distance 0 - 5

* Minimum acceptable score for competency:

  • Pass/Fail Tasks - Passing at least 70% of the objectives.
  • Graded Tasks - At least 2 on any single objective, and an overall average of at least 3.
